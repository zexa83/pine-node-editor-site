Features

      
Everything you need to prototype a strategy

      
A visual layer over Pine Script that keeps you in control of the logic, without touching syntax.

    

    

      

        
◆

        
Visual node canvas

        
Drag nodes onto the canvas and connect them like a flowchart — indicators, conditions, and actions all as reusable blocks.

      

      

        
≡

        
Full indicator library

        
RSI, MACD, EMA/SMA, Bollinger Bands, ATR and more — configure parameters directly on each node, no lookup tables needed.

      

      

        
⇄

        
Logic & conditions

        
Compare series, combine conditions with AND/OR, and branch entries and exits without writing a single if-statement.

      

      

        
▤

        
One-click Pine Script export

        
Compile your graph straight to Pine Script v6, ready to paste into TradingView's editor and run.

      

      

        
⤓

        
Save & reload graphs

        
Your strategy graphs are saved locally in the browser, so you can pick up exactly where you left off. Export to a file (Pro) if you want a portable backup.

      

      

        
⌘

        
Runs where you already work

        
A lightweight Chrome extension — no separate app to install, no account required to get started.

      

    

  






  

    

      
How it works

      
From idea to Pine Script in three steps

    

    

      

        
1

        
Drag in nodes

        
Pick a price source, add indicators like RSI or EMA, and drop them onto the canvas.

      

      

        
2

        
Wire up your logic

        
Connect outputs to conditions, combine them with AND/OR, and define your entry and exit rules visually.

      

      

        
3

        
Export & run

        
Compile to Pine Script v6 with one click and paste it straight into TradingView's Pine Editor.
